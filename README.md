## Abstract
This paper investigates the intersection between the Fibonacci sequence and Additive Number Theory, focusing specifically on the subset of even Fibonacci numbers, denoted by F(3k). Starting from additive anomalies involving modulo 11, we project Binet's Formula onto the finite field F_11 to establish strict topological constrictions. 

By adapting Chen's Theorem to this domain of exponential growth, we demonstrate that partitions (p, P_2) satisfying p + P_2 = F(3k) orbit modular frequencies dictated by the roots of the sequence's characteristic polynomial. Finally, we derive an unconditional asymptotic lower bound for the count of these structured partitions, quantifying the error term with rigorous analytic precision.
